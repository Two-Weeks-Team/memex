why it stays $0/mo to run

No chatbot. No model call at recall time. Your corpus, on your disk.

Memex is LLM-free at runtime: it reads your Claude Code / Codex session history and turns it into a navigable associative memory. The expensive thinking already happened when you lived through the work — Memex just keeps it and hands it back.

◇ $0 at recall

Surfaces, not searches

Proactive Recall watches your current context and lets the relevant past rise on its own. You're the recipient — there's no query to write and no token to spend when it lands.

▦ self-host

Local Qdrant, you own it

History becomes a vector space backed by Qdrant — Time Machine, Lens, Topology, Mix/Discovery. Run it on your machine or the one-image server / Docker variant. No SaaS seat to keep paying for.
