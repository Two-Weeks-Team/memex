What it is

Your sessions, as a space you move through.

Claude Code silently migrates and deletes old sessions. Memex reads your history — ~/.claude/projects/**/*.jsonl, legacy transcripts and history.jsonl — and keeps a corpus you own, backed by Qdrant. You move through it by similarity, not keywords.

No chatbot. No LLM call at runtime. Built for Qdrant Vector Space Day 2026 — “Think Outside the Bot.”

Straight answers

What this is — and isn't.

  • Is: a navigable spatial / associative memory over your own session history.
  • Isn't: a chatbot. There's no LLM call at runtime.
  • Storage: Qdrant vector store; the corpus stays yours.
  • Why: old sessions get migrated and deleted — this preserves them and lets you move by similarity.
