What's behind the surface

An associative memory you own — not a chatbot.

Claude Code silently migrates and deletes old sessions. Memex reads your local history — ~/.claude/projects/**/*.jsonl, legacy transcripts, and history.jsonl — and turns it into a navigable spatial memory backed by Qdrant. You travel by similarity, not keywords.
