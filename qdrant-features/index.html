TokenizerType::Word (default)✅ default-onUsed on both ai_title and ai_title_tokens. After client-side identifier expansion, the Word tokenizer correctly indexes each element.
TokenizerType::Whitespace⏸️ deferredNo use case in our corpus — Word handles punctuation better for natural-language titles.

No upstream feature request filed — client-side gives identical retrieval at zero external dependency.
